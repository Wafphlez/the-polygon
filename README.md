### Shoot, move, kill, die... You know the drill.

Fly around the small map, survive all the waves, defend yourself between the 2 portals, what constantly spawn 3 types of enemies:
- Interceptor – tries to get close.
- Inhibitor – decides where to spawn his minions.
- Sharpshooter – shoots from the distance.

```
Controls:
[WASD] - Move
[Mouse / Arrows] - Shoot
[Space] - Switch Weapon
```
